# Ecommerce_Sales_Analytics_Internship (Long Report)

# 1️⃣ Introduction
This internship project focused on analyzing ecommerce sales data using business-oriented analytical techniques and visualization methods. Multiple real-world business scenarios were solved using Excel, Python, SQL, and Looker Studio to understand sales performance, customer behavior, product demand, and category trends. The internship work was completed remotely under NullClass EdTech as part of practical analytics training and project implementation. This internship also provided real-time hands-on project experience in ecommerce sales analytics, dashboard development, SQL analysis, and business reporting workflows.

# 2️⃣ Objective
The main objective of this internship was to perform data analysis on ecommerce sales datasets and generate meaningful business insights using analytical tools and reporting techniques. The project aimed to improve practical skills in data filtering, sales comparison analysis, SQL querying, dashboard visualization, trend analysis, and business reporting.

# 3️⃣ Task 1 - Top5 Products 2022
This task focused on identifying the top 5 highest-selling products in the “Mobiles & Tablets” category during 2022 using valid order data. Python and Excel were used to filter and aggregate sales quantities product-wise. A horizontal bar chart was created using Matplotlib to visualize product performance and compare sales quantities clearly. The analysis highlighted products with the strongest customer demand during 2022.

# 4️⃣ Task 2 - Sales Decrease Analysis
This task analyzed the sales decrease in the “Others” product category between 2021 and 2022. Sales quantities from both years were filtered, merged, and compared to calculate quantity differences and percentage change. Product performance status was classified as DOWN, UP, or FAIR based on yearly comparison. Looker Studio dashboards and charts were developed to visualize product-wise sales decline trends and support business performance review.

# 5️⃣ Task 3 - Unpaid Checkout Customers
This task identified customers who completed the checkout process but did not complete payment transactions during 2022. Excel filtering techniques were used to retrieve customer records based on checkout and payment status conditions. Duplicate customer entries were removed to create a clean dataset for marketing follow-up analysis. The final output supports customer targeting and conversion-focused promotional activities.

# 6️⃣ Task 4 - Weekend Vs Weekday Sales
This task compared weekend and weekday sales performance during Q4 2022 using Python analysis and dashboard visualization techniques. Monthly average sales values were calculated for October, November, and December to evaluate promotional campaign effectiveness and customer purchase behavior. Looker Studio dashboards and Python charts were used to present sales trends and business insights clearly.

# 7️⃣ Task 5 - Top10 Sales Drop
This task focused on identifying the top 10 products with the highest sales decline between 2021 and 2022 using SQL analysis. MySQL queries were written to aggregate yearly sales quantities, calculate sales differences, and sort products based on the highest decline in performance. The results were visualized through Looker Studio dashboards, KPI scorecards, and comparison charts to support product performance evaluation and business analysis.

# 8️⃣ Task 6 - Category Sales Trend 2022
This task analyzed category-wise sales trends during 2022 using Excel pivot tables and time series visualization methods. Monthly sales quantities were aggregated across multiple product categories to compare category performance patterns and seasonal trends. A line chart visualization was created to present monthly category movement and identify categories with stronger sales activity during peak periods.

# 9️⃣ Key Outcomes
Through this internship project, practical experience was gained in sales data analysis, SQL querying, dashboard development, data visualization, and business insight generation. The project improved analytical thinking, reporting skills, and the ability to solve real-world ecommerce business problems using multiple analytical tools and technologies. The internship also strengthened understanding of real-time hands-on project workflows and business-oriented analytical reporting techniques.

# 🔟 Conclusion
The Ecommerce Sales Analytics Internship project successfully demonstrated the application of Excel, Python, SQL, and Looker Studio in solving business-focused analytical tasks. The internship provided hands-on exposure to sales analysis, customer behavior tracking, product performance evaluation, and trend visualization techniques. Overall, the project strengthened practical data analytics skills and improved understanding of real-world business reporting workflows through real-time hands-on project experience and analytical problem-solving activities.
