Insight Report — Top 5 Mobiles & Tablets Products by Sales Quantity (2022) - Task 1
The analysis identified the top-performing products in the “Mobiles & Tablets” category for 2022 by filtering only valid customer orders and aggregating product-wise sales quantities from the sales dataset. Product sales were grouped and ranked based on total quantity ordered to determine the highest-performing products within the category. Lenovo Pad P12 recorded the highest sales quantity, followed by Samsung Galaxy M14 5G and Vivo V29e, indicating stronger customer demand for these products during 2022. The results were visualized using a horizontal bar chart in Python, allowing easy comparison of product performance and sales distribution among the top products. This analysis helps the marketing and sales teams identify high-demand products, understand customer purchase trends, and support future promotional and inventory planning decisions.
